DAP District Reports provide monthly updates on harassment and displacement risk in rent stabilized buildings. Risk indicators include sales, HPD complaints, HPD violations, DOB complaints, and DOB construction permit applications.

Buildings that appear on multiple lists are coded yellow, orange, or red, depending on the level of risk.

Organizers have limited time and capacity, and we created DAP District Reports to make it easier to prioritize where to do outreach. These reports give regular updates on displacement risk to stabilized tenants in the neighborhoods where our organizers work.
